On the occasion of National Remembrance Day, Sokha emphasised, “I would like to pay my respects and pray for the souls of all Cambodians who lost their lives during the Democratic Kampuchea regime. May they rest in peace? On behalf of the next generation of Cambodians, I pledge to protect the peace that our leaders have worked so hard to achieve so that it will last forever and to firmly oppose the return of the Pol Pot genocidal regime.”
